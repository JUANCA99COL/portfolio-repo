Parkour App

The goal of this project was to develop a parkour app where young teenagers can practice parkour in a safe and fun way, allowing them to connect with more people and make part of a healthy community.

Users will be able to chat and share photos of their progress and experience with their friends. They will have the chance to improve their skills over time and book parkour classes with the help of instructors.
